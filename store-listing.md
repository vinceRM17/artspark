## Description

ArtSpark delivers a personalized art prompt to your screen every day, tailored to your preferred mediums, subjects, and skill level. Whether you work in watercolor, pencil, digital, or mixed media, each prompt is crafted to push your creative boundaries while staying within your comfort zone.

DAILY PERSONALIZED PROMPTS
Get a fresh creative prompt every day, matched to your art supplies and interests. Each prompt includes a medium, subject, and optional creative twist to keep things interesting.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Build streaks, earn badges, and watch your creative practice grow. Review your prompt history and see how far you've come.

CREATIVE CHALLENGES
Join multi-day challenges like "7 Days of Nature" or "30 Days of Portraits" to develop specific skills and build consistency.

PORTFOLIO & GALLERY
Photograph your artwork, add notes and tags, and build a personal gallery of everything you create. Your photos are yours — we never share them with AI or third parties.

WORKS OFFLINE
Create and save responses even without internet. Everything syncs when you reconnect.

FEATURES
- Personalized daily prompts based on your mediums and subjects
- Visual reference images for inspiration
- Creative challenges with daily checklists
- Streak tracking and achievement badges
- Photo portfolio with notes and tags
- Light and dark mode
- Beginner tutorials and learning resources
- Weekly creative recaps
- Bookmark your favorite prompts
- Customizable notification reminders

ArtSpark is made for artists of every level — from curious beginners to working professionals. Start your daily creative practice today.
